History of Mussoorie

Mussoorie: The Queen of the Hills
Mussoorie has long been known as the Queen of the Hills. The name Mussoorie is often attributed to a derivation of mansūr, a shrub which is indigenous to the area. The town is often referred to as Mansuri by Indians.
Gorkha Conquest and Establishment
In 1803, the Gorkhas under Umer Singh Thapa conquered the Garhwal and the Dehra, whereby Mussoorie was established.
Colonial Brewing Tradition
Due to its colonial past, Mussoorie has a long tradition of brewing. India’s first brewing house ‘The Old Brewery’ was started by Sir Henry Bohle in Mussoorie.
