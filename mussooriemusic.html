Traditional Music of Uttarakhand

Garhwali Music
Garhwali music is characterized by its melodious tunes and simple yet soulful lyrics. It often revolves around themes of nature, love, and day-to-day life in the hills.
Popular Instruments:
  • Dhol
  • Damau
  • Ransingha
  • Flute
Kumaoni Music
Kumaoni music is known for its rhythmic beats and vivid expressions. The songs often narrate tales of local heroes, deities, and natural beauty.
Popular Instruments:
  • Chholiya
  • Dhol
  • Nagara
  • Hurka
Jagar
Jagar is a traditional form of musical storytelling that involves invoking local deities and spirits through songs and rituals.
Bhagat Songs
These are songs sung by local spiritual leaders (bhagats) during religious ceremonies and gatherings.
