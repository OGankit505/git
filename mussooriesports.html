Popular Sports of Uttarakhand

Dhopkhel
Dhopkhel is a traditional team sport played in Uttarakhand. It involves hitting a wooden ball with a long wooden stick and is popular in rural areas of the state.
Gilli Danda
Gilli Danda is a widely popular traditional sport in Uttarakhand and across India. It involves hitting a small wooden gilli (stick) with a larger danda (stick) and is played in both rural and urban areas.
