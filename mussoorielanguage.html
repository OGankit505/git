Languages Spoken in Mussoorie

Garhwali
The main language spoken by the people of Mussoorie is Garhwali. It is the regional language of the Garhwal region and holds cultural significance for the locals.
Hindi
In addition to Garhwali, Hindi is also widely spoken in Mussoorie. It serves as a common language for communication, education, and business in the area.
Punjabi
Punjabi is spoken by a significant population in Mussoorie, contributing to the cultural diversity of the region. It is especially prevalent among the Punjabi-speaking community.
Kumaoni
Kumaoni is another regional language spoken in parts of Mussoorie, particularly by those who belong to the Kumaon region of Uttarakhand.
