Traditional Foods of Uttarakhand

Phannu
Phannu is a traditional dish made from mixed lentils, usually black gram, green gram, and bengal gram. It is slow-cooked to a thick consistency and seasoned with local spices.
Chainsoo
Chainsoo is a spicy dish made from black gram (urad dal) and is a staple in Uttarakhand cuisine. It is often served with rice or roti.
Sisunak Saag
Sisunak Saag is a local green leafy vegetable dish made from the leaves of the sisunak plant. It is cooked with local spices and often served with roti or rice.
Thechwani
Thechwani is a flavorful dish made from potatoes and radishes, pounded together with local spices. It is typically enjoyed with steamed rice or roti.
Kabab
Kabab is a popular dish in Uttarakhand, usually made from minced meat (such as mutton or chicken) mixed with various spices and grilled or fried to perfection.
Garhwal ka Fanna
Garhwal ka Fanna is a traditional dish from the Garhwal region made from baby bamboo shoots. It is cooked with local spices and often enjoyed with rice.
Bhang ki Chutney
Bhang ki Chutney is a unique chutney made from hemp seeds. It has a distinct nutty flavor and is a popular condiment in Uttarakhand cuisine.
Singori
Singori is a sweet delicacy made from khoya (condensed milk) wrapped in malu leaves. It is a popular treat in the Kumaon region of Uttarakhand.
Bal Mithai
Bal Mithai is a sweet confection made from roasted khoya and coated with white sugar balls. It is a beloved dessert in Uttarakhand.
Chana Madra
Chana Madra is a rich and creamy dish made from chickpeas cooked in a yogurt-based gravy. It is a flavorful delight in Uttarakhand cuisine.
