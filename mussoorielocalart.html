Traditional and Local Art of Uttarakhand

Pahadi Paintings
These are intricate, colorful paintings that depict scenes from nature, local folklore, and daily life in the hills.
Garhwal Paintings
Garhwali paintings are one of the best ways of learning more about the culture here. They are also one of the most loved souvenirs in Mussoorie.
Wood Carvings
Mussoorie also produces some interesting, traditional wooden dolls called Pahadi Juniyali dolls. The excellent wood carvings can also be seen in the temple walls and gates here.
Dekara
Dekaras are clay modeled figurines of gods and goddesses created by hand in three-dimensional forms.
Bhagwati Masks
These are wooden masks that represent various deities and are used in religious ceremonies and festivals.
