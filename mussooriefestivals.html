Traditional Festivals of Mussoorie

Bikhauti Festival
This is an important festival celebrated in the Kumaon region, including parts of Mussoorie. It marks the victory of the Goddess against the demon king and is celebrated with great fervor.
Harela and Bhitauli
These are agricultural festivals celebrated twice a year (in monsoon and spring) to mark the sowing and harvesting seasons. People decorate their homes with greenery and exchange gifts.
Basant Panchami
Celebrated during the spring season, this festival is dedicated to the goddess of knowledge, Saraswati. It is marked by prayers, feasts, and cultural events.
Phool Dei
This is a unique festival celebrated in the Kumaon region, including parts of Mussoorie. It involves the exchange of flowers and gifts among families to bring prosperity and good luck.
Ganga Dussehra
Celebrated in honor of the River Ganges, this festival marks the day when the Ganges descended to Earth. Pilgrims from Mussoorie often visit the holy city of Haridwar to take part in the celebrations.
