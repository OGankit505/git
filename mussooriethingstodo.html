Things to Do in Mussoorie

Sunset at Lal Tibba
Enjoy a breathtaking sunset at Lal Tibba, the highest point in Mussoorie. It offers panoramic views of the Himalayan range and is a must-visit for nature enthusiasts and photographers.
Watersports at Kempty Falls
Indulge in thrilling water sports activities at Kempty Falls. From boating to river rafting, it's a perfect destination for adventure seekers looking for some aquatic fun.
Trekking in Nag Tibba Summit
Embark on an exciting trek to Nag Tibba Summit and experience the beauty of the Garhwal region. It's a popular trekking destination, offering stunning views and a sense of adventure.
Benog Hill Exploration
Explore the scenic Benog Hill and soak in the serene ambiance of nature. It's an ideal spot for nature walks and birdwatching, providing a tranquil escape from city life.
Jharipani Waterfall Trek
Embark on a trek to Jharipani Waterfall, a mesmerizing cascade surrounded by lush greenery. It's a perfect outing for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.
Visit to Benog Wildlife Sanctuary
Discover the rich biodiversity of Mussoorie at the Benog Wildlife Sanctuary. Explore the forested trails and catch glimpses of various flora and fauna species in their natural habitat.
Paragliding Adventure
Experience the thrill of paragliding and soar through the skies of Mussoorie. It's an exhilarating activity that offers a unique perspective of the hills and valleys below.
